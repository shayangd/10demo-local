### Key Findings

1. **Strong Problem Validation**: 91% of interviewees experience the demo scheduling/qualification problem within the last 90 days with high pain intensity (7.18/10). Nearly all (91%) have active workarounds consuming significant resources.

2. **Clear Solution-Market Fit**: 64% willing to switch from current solutions, and 73% see clear outcome alignment. However, solution interest slightly below threshold (54.5%) due to concerns about AI replacing nuanced human discovery calls.

3. **Validated Willingness to Pay**: 91% have confirmed budgets and are currently spending on sales teams/SDRs. Optimal price range validated at $200-$500/month, with high-volume users willing to pay $500-$1,000/month.

- Live AI calls with screen sharing
- Full demo capabilities
- Target: 30-100 demos/month

**Tier 3 - Enterprise/High Volume** ($999-1,999/month or usage-based):
- Multiple agents
- 100+ demos/month
- Advanced features

**Analysis**: Strong validation of $200-$500 optimal price range with clear willingness to pay up to $1,000/month for high-volume users. No one cited pricing as "too expensive" in the $200-500 range.
